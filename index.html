A Little bit About
PRMI Bandung

Peña Real Madrid de Indonesia Bandung is a community of Real Madrid supporters based in Bandung City. Founded cause the love of the Real Madrid C.F., PRMI Bandung was officially established on August 18, 2009. Currently, we have many activities such as NoMad / NontonMadrid (Watching Real Madrid matches together), Fun Futsal, and Fun Mini Soccer. PRMI Bandung has an official membership that is integrated with Pena Real Madrid de Indonesia.
